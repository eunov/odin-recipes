Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, the dense marinara cake of Italy. An emalgimation of sauce, cheese and pasta, laid on top of one another in repeated succession that can only be described as as close to heaven as you can get. The layers of sauce and cheese sticking to the inside of your ribcage, like italian molasses. Do not fret, however, for this dish may require a nap afterwards and you will not be able to walk as fast, for the experience of slamming down a corner piece of lasagna is worth the slowed penalty. I, for one, would slam lasagna every night if it were feesible, or healthy.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Egg
Meat Sauce
  • Onion, fine diced
  • Ground beef
  • Ground italian sausage
  • Garlic
  • Chicken broth
  • Marinara sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Worcetershire sauce
Lasagna Noodles
  • Lasagna noodles
  • More mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Combine cheese filling
  2. Heat a pan and add oil, add onions until soft.
  3. Add sausage and ground beef
  4. Add chicken broth.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenehit.
  6. Begin boiling a pot of water and cook the noodles to al dente.
  7. Spread meat sauce on the bottom, then add 4 lasagna noodles.
  8. Spread 1/3 of the cheese mixture, and then more meat sauce.
  9. Add more noodles, and repeat until each mixture is gone.
  10. Top with mozerrella cheese.
  11. Cover and bake for 35 minutes, then remove the cover and bake for another 10 minutes
