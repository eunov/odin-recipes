Taco Recipe

Description

Tacos, one of the most recognizable dishes across North America and beyond. Originating from Mexico, these tortillas filled with various grilled meats, onions, cilantro, and lime can be smelled a mile away. A great dinner, or even something to help soak up the grease, tacos will be great with anything.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Al Pastor, Carne Asada, or Pollo
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Limes
  1. Grill your meat of choice
  2. While the meat is cooking, dice up an onion, chop your cliantro, and cut limes into quarters.
  3. Once everything is cooked and diced, cook your tortillas on a comal or a pan.
  4. Flip once after you can see some bubbling, or cooked spots on the back of the torilla.
  5. Once tortillas are cooked, add your meat, then sprinkle on diced onions and cilantro.
  6. Give a squeeze of lime, and add a lime to garnish.
