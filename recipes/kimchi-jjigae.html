Kimchi Jjigae

Description

Kimchi Jjigae, also known as kimchi stew, is a delicacy found in hearts of Koreans everywhere. This noursihing, spicy stew can make you feel whole again after a long day. Filled with various vegetables, meats, and flavors, this is a recipe for the entire family to enjoy - but please, make sure to make it right, and use the proper ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Kimchi (the sourer, better)
  • Kimchi brine
  • Protein of choice: pork and tofu are great options!
  • Green onions
  • White onion
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Gochugaru
  • Gochujang
  • Sesame oil
  • Anchovy stock

Steps

  1. Place kimchi and kimchi brine into a shallow pot
  2. Add protein (that is not tofu!) and white onion
  3. Slice green onions and add them to the pot.
  4. Add salt, sugar, Gochugaru, and Gochujang. Drizzle sesame oil over top and add stock.
  5. Cover and cook for 10 minutes over medium high heat.
  6. Open and mix with a spoon, and lay tofu over top.
  7. Cover and cook for another 10 minutes.
  8. Chop a green onion and put it on top of stew. Remove from heat and serve with rice!
